Our Story

Born from a simple observation: homeowners were losing money, time, and peace of mind because they didn't have a complete picture of their home's needs and history.

The Problem We Solved

After years of working in home improvement and real estate, we noticed a pattern: homeowners were constantly surprised by maintenance issues, warranty expirations, and missed opportunities to increase their property value.

People were spending thousands on emergency repairs that could have been prevented, losing money on expired warranties, and missing out on improvements that would significantly increase their home's value.

We realized that the solution wasn't just better tools—it was a complete system that would give homeowners the same level of insight and control that professional property managers have.

The Lightbulb Moment

"What if every homeowner could have a complete digital record of their home, with AI-powered insights and professional services to keep everything running perfectly?"

Our Mission

To empower every homeowner with the tools, insights, and services they need to maximize their home's value while minimizing stress and unexpected costs.

Complete Home Intelligence

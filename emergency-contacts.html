Emergency Procedures

Step-by-step procedures for common emergencies

Fire Emergency

  1. Evacuate immediately - don't try to save belongings
  2. Call 911 from outside the building
  3. Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation
  4. Meet at designated meeting point
  5. Do not re-enter until cleared by firefighters

Gas Leak

  1. Evacuate immediately - don't use phones or electronics
  2. Call gas company from outside: 1-800-GAS-LEAK
  3. Do not turn on/off any electrical switches
  4. Stay away from the building
  5. Wait for gas company to arrive

Water Leak/Flood

  1. Turn off main water valve immediately
  2. Turn off electricity if water near electrical
  3. Call emergency plumber
  4. Document damage with photos
  5. Contact insurance company

Power Outage

  1. Check if neighbors have power
  2. Call power company: 1-800-POWER
  3. Unplug sensitive electronics
  4. Use flashlights, not candles
  5. Keep refrigerator/freezer closed

Medical Emergency

  1. Call 911 immediately
  2. Stay with the person
  3. Follow dispatcher instructions
  4. Gather medical information
  5. Clear path for emergency vehicles

Break-in/Security

  1. Call 911 immediately
  2. Do not confront intruders
  3. Stay in safe room if inside
  4. Document everything for police
  5. Contact insurance company

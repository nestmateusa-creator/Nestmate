Everything You Need to Manage Your Home

NestMate tracks every detail of your home, from the smallest trim piece to major systems. Here's exactly what you get with your subscription.

Home Structure & Details

  • Window Sizes & Types: Track every window's dimensions, style, and installation date
  • Trim Styles & Materials: Record crown molding, baseboards, door casings, and materials used
  • Flooring Details: Document room-by-room flooring types, installation dates, and warranties
  • Paint Colors & Brands: Store paint codes, brands, and application dates for every room
  • Door Specifications: Track door sizes, styles, hardware, and security features
  • Ceiling Details: Record ceiling heights, materials, and any special features
  • Wall Materials: Document drywall, plaster, paneling, and insulation details

Systems & Appliances

  • HVAC System: Track model numbers, installation dates, maintenance history, and warranties
  • Plumbing System: Document pipe materials, fixture brands, and water pressure details
  • Electrical System: Record panel capacity, outlet types, and electrical upgrades
  • Water Heater: Track capacity, fuel type, age, and maintenance schedule
  • Kitchen Appliances: Document all appliances with model numbers and warranties
  • Laundry Equipment: Track washer/dryer specifications and maintenance needs
  • Security Systems: Record alarm systems, cameras, and smart home devices

Exterior & Landscaping

  • Roof Details: Track shingle type, installation date, and warranty information
  • Siding Materials: Document siding type, color, and maintenance schedule
  • Gutters & Downspouts: Record materials, installation dates, and cleaning schedule
  • Driveway & Walkways: Track materials, dimensions, and maintenance needs
  • Landscaping: Document trees, shrubs, irrigation systems, and maintenance schedules
  • Outdoor Structures: Record sheds, decks, patios, and their maintenance needs
  • Fencing: Track fence materials, heights, and maintenance requirements

Maintenance & Service

  • Service Records: Complete history of all maintenance, repairs, and improvements
  • Warranty Tracking: Monitor all warranties and their expiration dates
  • Contractor Database: Store contact info for all service providers
  • Maintenance Schedules: Automated reminders for seasonal and routine maintenance
  • Cost Tracking: Record all home-related expenses and improvements
  • Receipt Storage: Digital storage for all home-related receipts and invoices
  • Emergency Contacts: Quick access to emergency services and trusted contractors

AI-Powered Smart Features

  • Health Score: AI-calculated home health score based on maintenance and age
  • Predictive Maintenance: AI predicts when systems need attention before they fail
  • Value Tracking: Monitor your home's value and ROI on improvements
  • Product Recommendations: AI suggests products based on your home's specific needs
  • Price Alerts: Get notified when products you need go on sale
  • Voice Assistant: Control your home management with voice commands
  • Smart Reminders: Intelligent scheduling based on your home's unique characteristics

Family & Collaboration

  • Family Management: Invite family members to collaborate on home management
  • Task Assignment: Assign maintenance tasks to family members
  • Shared Calendar: Family-wide calendar for home maintenance and events
  • Team Product Orders: Collaborate on purchasing decisions for home improvements
  • Family Dashboard: Centralized view of all family home management activities
  • Emergency Services: Quick access to emergency contacts and procedures
  • Communication Hub: Family messaging and notification system

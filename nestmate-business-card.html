Print Instructions

For Professional Printing:

  • Use 16pt or 18pt cardstock
  • Print at 300 DPI for best quality
  • Standard business card size: 3.5" × 2"
  • Bleed area: 0.125" on all sides

For Home Printing:

  • Use Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac)
  • Select "More settings" → "Paper size: Custom"
  • Set dimensions to 3.5" × 2"
  • Use high-quality photo paper

Digital Use:

  • Save as PDF for email signatures
  • Export as PNG for social media
  • Use in presentations and proposals

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NestMate home management

What is NestMate?

NestMate is a comprehensive home management platform that helps you organize, track, and maintain your home. From basic home details to advanced family collaboration, NestMate provides everything you need to keep your home in perfect condition.

What are the different pricing plans?

We offer three plans: Basic ($5/month) for single home management, Pro ($10/month) for unlimited homes and advanced features, and Advanced Pro ($16/month) for family collaboration and premium services. All plans include a 30-day free trial.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

You can try any of our plans for 30 days completely free. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all features of your chosen plan during the trial period.

Can I manage multiple homes?

Yes! Pro and Advanced Pro plans allow you to manage unlimited homes. Basic plan is limited to one home. Perfect for property managers, real estate investors, or families with multiple properties.

What is the Home Health Score?

The Home Health Score is a comprehensive assessment of your home's condition across multiple categories including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, structural, safety, and energy efficiency. Available in Pro and Advanced Pro plans.

How does family collaboration work?

Advanced Pro plan allows up to 5 family members to collaborate. Share tasks, manage budgets together, assign responsibilities, and track family activity in real-time through our family dashboard.

Can I export my data?

Yes! All plans include data export capabilities. Basic plan offers standard export, while Pro and Advanced Pro plans include advanced export options with detailed reports and analytics.

What kind of support do you offer?

Basic plan includes standard support. Pro plan offers VIP priority support for faster response times. Advanced Pro plan provides family-first support with dedicated assistance for family collaboration features.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage, secure authentication, and regular backups. Your home data is protected with the same security standards used by major financial institutions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Getting Started

What is NestMate home management software and how does it work?

NestMate is a comprehensive home management platform designed for homeowners, property managers, and real estate professionals. Our smart home organization system helps you track every detail of your property including maintenance schedules, warranty information, home improvements, and property documentation.

NestMate combines AI-powered insights with professional services to help you increase your property value by 15% or more through proper documentation and proactive maintenance. Our residential property management software features include maintenance reminders, home health scores, budget tracking, and family collaboration tools.

How do I create a NestMate account and start managing my home?

Getting started with NestMate home organization software is simple:

  • Click "Get Started" and choose your property management plan (Basic, Pro, or Advanced Pro)
  • Create your account with email and password for secure home data storage
  • Enter your home details including property information, square footage, and year built
  • Start adding maintenance records, warranty information, and home improvement documentation
  • Set up AI-powered maintenance reminders and track your home health score

Basic plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to Basic features. No credit card required for the trial period.

What's the difference between Basic, Pro, and Advanced Pro plans?

Basic Plan ($5/month): Perfect for single-home management. Includes basic home tracking, 10 tasks, service records, warranty tracking, and standard support. Ideal for first-time homeowners and apartment dwellers.

Pro Plan ($10/month): Best for serious homeowners and property managers. Features unlimited homes, unlimited tasks, home health scores, AI-powered insights, price tracking, professional floor plans, Amazon product recommendations, and VIP support.

Advanced Pro Plan ($16/month): Ultimate property management solution. Includes everything in Pro plus family collaboration (5 members), advanced analytics, premium export options, priority technician services, and dedicated family support.

Features & Functionality

What is the Home Health Score and how does it help my property maintenance?

The Home Health Score is our AI-powered property assessment system that analyzes your home's condition across multiple categories:

  • HVAC Systems: Heating, cooling, and ventilation maintenance tracking
  • Plumbing: Water systems, pipes, and fixture condition monitoring
  • Electrical: Wiring, outlets, and electrical safety assessment
  • Structural: Foundation, roof, walls, and building integrity
  • Safety: Smoke detectors, carbon monoxide, and security systems
  • Energy Efficiency: Insulation, windows, and utility optimization

Your Home Health Score uses predictive analytics to identify potential issues before they become expensive repairs, helping homeowners save an average of $847 annually on maintenance costs.

How does NestMate's AI-powered maintenance prediction work?

NestMate's smart maintenance system uses machine learning algorithms with 94% prediction accuracy to analyze your property data:

  • Analyzes maintenance history and equipment age to predict future service needs
  • Sends automated reminders before warranties expire (saving thousands in replacement costs)
  • Identifies seasonal maintenance requirements for optimal home performance
  • Detects patterns that indicate potential issues (like HVAC efficiency decline)
  • Recommends cost-effective improvements to increase property value

Our AI system learns from over 15,000 homes managed on NestMate, providing you with data-driven insights for proactive property care.

Can I create and save floor plans for my home or rental property?

Yes! NestMate includes professional floor plan features:

  • Basic Plan: Upload existing floor plans and property blueprints
  • Pro & Advanced Pro Plans: Interactive floor plan designer with grid-style drawing, architectural symbols for doors and windows, furniture placement tools, electrical outlet mapping, dimension lines (feet/inches), curved wall support, and 3D view capability

Use floor plans for home renovation planning, room measurements, furniture arrangement, contractor communication, and property documentation. Save multiple versions and track changes over time.

How does family collaboration work for shared home management?

Advanced Pro plan includes family collaboration features for up to 5 members:

  • Shared access to all home data, maintenance records, and documents
  • Assign tasks to family members with automated notifications
  • Track who completed which maintenance activities
  • Shared budget tracking and expense management
  • Family activity log for transparency and accountability
  • Individual permissions for sensitive financial information

Perfect for couples, families with adult children, or multigenerational homes managing property together.

Home Management Checklists

Spring Maintenance

Inspect and clean gutters and downspouts
Service HVAC system and replace filters
Check roof for winter damage
Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
Inspect exterior paint and siding
Clean and inspect outdoor equipment

Summer Maintenance

Inspect and clean air conditioning units
Check irrigation systems and sprinklers
Inspect windows and doors for energy efficiency
Clean and maintain outdoor living spaces
Check electrical systems and outlets
Inspect foundation and drainage systems

Fall Maintenance

Clean gutters and prepare for winter
Service heating system and replace filters
Inspect and clean chimney and fireplace
Check weatherstripping and insulation
Prepare outdoor equipment for storage
Test emergency systems and backup power

Winter Maintenance

Monitor heating system performance
Check for ice dams and roof issues
Inspect pipes for freezing risks
Test carbon monoxide detectors
Check insulation and energy efficiency
Plan spring maintenance and improvements
